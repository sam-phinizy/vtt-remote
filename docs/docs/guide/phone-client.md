# Phone Client

The phone client is a mobile-optimized web app for controlling your VTT session.

## Interface Overview

The client presents a clean, touch-friendly interface with:

- **Quick Actions** - Common operations at your fingertips
- **Dice Roller** - Tap to roll, swipe for modifiers
- **Macro Grid** - Your Foundry macros, one tap away
